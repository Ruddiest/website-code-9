Basic Maintenance Checklist

Regular maintenance is essential for keeping your dirt bike in top condition. Here's a basic checklist:

  • Check tire pressure
  • Lubricate the chain
  • Inspect brakes
  • Clean and replace air filter
  • Check engine oil level

Common Repairs

Even with regular maintenance, minor repairs may be necessary. Common issues include flat tires, spark plug replacement, and brake adjustments. Learning basic repair skills can save you time and money.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter problems with your dirt bike, try these troubleshooting tips:

  • Check for loose bolts or connections
  • Inspect fuel lines for leaks
  • Consult your owner's manual for troubleshooting guides
